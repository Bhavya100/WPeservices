Our platform will allow you to do three things:
    1. Find venues/vendors, you may not know of, based on your specific requirements. Not just that, we can provide you with answers to all your questions about the particular venue/vendor, including pricing information.

    3. Get negotiated prices across venue/vendors categories in order to make an informed decision. We take away the hassle of individually negotiating with several venues and vendors in order to plan your event. With White Pocket , you make the decision and we make it right for you.
